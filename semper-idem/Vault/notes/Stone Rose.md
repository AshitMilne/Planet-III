The Stone Rose is a metaphor for the layers of habits that are put or pat down by a person that  dictate behaviour. These are analogous to an archeological mound a tel (תל) or a tal (تَلّ) that denotes layers of human settlement. A culture rose, flowered and left its mark in stone. My birth name pronounced 'Put-Tel' denotes the construction of my own tel. With Act III I am settled in who I am - adopting the name Milne (मिलना) meaning 'come together or meet' in Hindi. The lingua franca along with English of my ancestral India. I now distill to clarify who I am so that I may burn cleanly at the end.
